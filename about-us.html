Street Architecture and Urbanism

St. A&U (Street Architecture and Urbanism) is an architecture firm based in Indonesia and was founded in 2018 by menu-sekunder-three young architect; Ms. Hasballah, Ms. Cantika, and Ms. Istiqamah. As architects and designers, we devoted to creating (magnificent/ effective and efficient) spaces for better living which are socially and environmentally responsible. The purpose of Street Architecture and Urbanism (St.A&U) is to enable communities to architectural services and continuing research on architecture and urban-related issues in order to optimize the vital role of architecture in the people's lens. St. A&U is mostly interested in creating design solutions while embedding societal as well as environmental concepts in the design process, particularly on urbanism context.
